# Forecasting & Time Series Analytics

## Overview
This project applies time series analysis and forecasting techniques to analyze historical data and generate future projections for business planning and decision-making.

The focus is on practical forecasting methods commonly used in operations, finance, and strategic analytics.

## Business Problem
Organizations rely on forecasts to:
- Anticipate demand and seasonality
- Plan inventory and capacity
- Support budgeting and long-term strategy

Accurate forecasting transforms historical data into actionable insights.

## Solution Approach
- Explored time series components (trend, seasonality, noise)
- Applied classical forecasting techniques
- Evaluated forecast accuracy and model behavior
- Interpreted results in a business context
